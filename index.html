Hello.

I am a designer with a background in architecture and urbanism. After graduating with a Master of Architecture degree from University College Dublin in my native Ireland, I spent three years working for architectural firms in New York City, before taking a step back to explore my interests in urbanism, planning, and larger-scale design with an internship at the NYC Department of City Planning.

Right now.

I have returned to Europe following my work as Zoning Analyst and Illustrator at Envelope, a software company spun out of a collaboration between SHoP Architects and the Director of MIT's Civic Data Design Lab. I am interested in architecture, planning, and technology, and I currently seek new opportunities in these fields. If you would like to talk, please don't hesitate to reach out.
