Hello.

I am an Irish designer focused on the intersection of architecture, urban design, and technology. I am professionally trained as an architect, earning my Master of Architecture degree from University College Dublin, Ireland, and more recently graduating with a Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Practice from the University of Westminster, London. I have experience working with architectural firms in Germany, China, the UK, the USA, and Ireland..

Right now.

I am currently based in London, where I work as Architect at FBM, a practice focussed on projects in the residential and educational sectors. I recently completed the design of a new Collaborative Hub for IBM and am currently working on the design of an Institute of Productivity for the University of East Anglia. Outside of this, I pursue my interests in urbanism and technology through contract work at Envelope, an urban mapping startup formed out of SHoP Architects and based in New York.
