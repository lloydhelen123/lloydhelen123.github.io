Spenner Zement HQ

First prize competition win for the headquarters of a cement manufacturer

Project Date
2011
Location
Erwitte, Germany
Role
Architectural Designer
Organization
BOLLES-WILSON
Team Members
Peter Wilson, Franziska Lindinger, Miguel Anglel Diaz, Langzi Chiu, Eric Halter (AHW Engineers)

BOLLES-WILSON was selected from an invited competition to design a new heaquarters for Spenner Zement GmbH. The site was located within protected woodlands, which meant strict restrictions on the size of the building footprint. The design concept elevates the bulk of the building above the ground plane by way of concrete 'mega-trusses' enabling a dramatic cantilevered structure. This minimizes the ground floor footprint while also taking advantage of the prime natural views into the landscape beyond.

The Beam is the Scheme

Expressive concrete beams enable the dramatic thireen-metre cantilever. Our team worked closely with the structural engineer, Eric Halter, using a parametric optimizing program called Softkill to remove redundant concrete as shown in the diagram opposite. This enabled the design of the slimmest possible concrete truss beam.

Sustainable Design

The design incorporates numerous sustainable design strategies to mitigate the environmental impact of the building, including:

  • Geothermal heating
  • Electrical solar panels
  • Passive cooling
  • Rainwater harvesting
