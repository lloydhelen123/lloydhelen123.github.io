Hello.

I'm an architect and urbanist currently based in New York City. After graduating with a Master of Architecture degree from University College Dublin in my native Ireland, I spent three years working for architectural firms in NYC, before recently taking a step back to explore my interests in urbanism, planning, and larger-scale design, with an internship at the NYC Department of City Planning.

Right now.

I work as Zoning Analyst and Tester at Envelope, a software company spun out of long-time collaboration between the SHoP Architects and the Director of MIT's Civic Data Design Lab. I am a Young Fellow of the Urban Design Forum, and enjoy attending events by MAS and the Architectural League. In my spare time I run the streets of Brooklyn with North Brooklyn Runners, and play around making websites, like this one.
